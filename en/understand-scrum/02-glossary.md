# Scrum Glossary & Terminology

Key terms and definitions used in our Scrum implementation.

## Roles

| Term | Full Name | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PO** | Product Owner | Owns the product vision, manages the backlog, and prioritizes work based on value (ROI). |
| **SM** | Scrum Master | Facilitates Scrum ceremonies, removes impediments, and coaches the team on Agile practices. |
| **BA** | Business Analyst | Bridges the gap between business and technology, analyzes requirements, and writes user stories. |
| **PM** | Project Manager | Manages timeline, budget, resources, risk, and cross-team coordination. |
| **Tech Lead** | Technical Lead | Guides architectural decisions, code quality, mentoring, and technical debt management. |
| **QA** | Quality Assurance | Focuses on test strategy, automation, and ensuring quality standards. |
